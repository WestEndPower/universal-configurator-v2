# Universal Configurator V3.1.0

## Universal Dealer Rules Engine
- Loads optional `data/dealer-rules.csv`.
- Supports profit, margin, discount, approval, selling-price, warning, and blocking rules.
- Filters by dealer, location, brand, product, SKU, and category.
- Adds standardized PASS, WARNING, APPROVAL_REQUIRED, BLOCKED, and SKIPPED outcomes.
- Adds Dealer Rules Inspector tab and diagnostics export data.
- Missing dealer-rules.csv is neutral and does not stop startup.
- Dealer settings and existing dealer rule data are not included or overwritten.
